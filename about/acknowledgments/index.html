Fonts

For typomaniacs, the typeface for Latin scripts is PT Serif, a transitional serif typeface with humanistic terminals, designed by ParaType. From my experience, it is one of the best rendered free serif faces on FreeType.

The preferred CJK typeface on this site is Hanazono Mincho.
